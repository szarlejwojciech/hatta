## Practicing interior architecture requires designers to consider pretty much everything to do with the building of an...

...interior space including materials, finishes, electrical requirements, plumbing, lighting, ventilation, and intelligent use of space amongst other things that affect humans (in and around).

Designers with training in interior architecture would usually be present at, and have an important say during all stages of the construction process, from the initial plans right through to the finishing touches.
